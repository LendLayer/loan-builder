Insight Loan Builder

Hey guys!

Based on our conversation last week, here's a little loan calculator that gives you an idea of what I think we can offer. With interest rates, ideally we'd start off a bit more cautiously, and then over 6 months as we get more data, improve our models and diversify properly, we can be more aggressive and further reduce interest rates on a student-by-student basis.

The Model

Essentially we can put together whatever package works for the fellows and for Insight, and this is just a simple model to get us started. In this model, the interest is paid up-front by Insight and is calculated by summing up the net present values of all the future loan payments (positive and negative). If you're interested, the code is here.

Interest Rates

Assuming Insight were to guarantee some proportion, say the first 20%, of students that might default, you might think of our risk as the risk that up to 20% of students can't pay and Insight can't pay, plus the risk that the rest of the 80% can't pay. In this scenario, we'd ideally sit at around 6% interest.

If we took on the risk from the students, until we got good at this and had some hard data, I'd rather stay a bit conservative for the first cohort so we don't get wiped off the planet. Ideally we'd be in the 16% APR range. My instinct is that less than 10% of students would default, but there's a hefty downside for us if more than that do, because we'd report small or negative earnings on our first fund, and I guess most of our future investment would dry up.
